Community of Practice: Program Operations and Optimization

Center for Academic Innovation, 317 Maynard Street, Ann Arbor, Michigan, 48104, United States
Wednesday, November 12, 2025, 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM ET

After launching a program and enrolling students, the work of a successful program doesn’t stop - it evolves. The Center for Academic Innovation’s current call for proposals emphasizes not only developing new programs, but also optimizing existing ones to enhance student experience, increase operational efficiency, and expand impact. Join this session to hear about programs that are taking steps to optimize their operations. Resources for thinking through ongoing operations will be shared. Learn more about the types of potential support you can propose in the current call for proposals.


This session benefits both those interested in developing new programs and previous partners looking to optimize their existing programs and expand their impact.


We encourage in-person attendance, however if you are unable to make it, you may register to attend virtually. Light snacks and refreshments provided.
