Innovation Summit: AI & the Future of Learning

Michigan Union, 530 S. State Street, Ann Arbor, MI, 48109
Tuesday, March 17, 2026, 8:30 AM to 2:00 PM ET

Join students, faculty, and campus partners, including industry leaders and peer institutions, to explore the intersection of advanced technology and higher education.


As part of the University of Michigan Life-Changing Education theme year and Campus of the Future event series, the AI & the Future of Learning Summit will bring together the brightest minds in education and the latest advanced technologies, including artificial intelligence tools, that are reshaping our academic landscape.


Join us Tuesday, March 17, from 8:30 a.m. - 2 p.m. in the Rogel Ballroom at the Michigan Union. The summit, hosted by the Center for Academic Innovation, is both a showcase and a working space. It is an opportunity to learn from one another, test ideas, and engage with the real questions facing universities today. Participants will engage in:

  • Collaborative Dialogue: Lightning talks and panel discussions exploring the opportunities and challenges of university–industry partnerships and the evolving role of AI in teaching, learning, and assessment.
  • Student-Centered Perspectives: Hear from students who are navigating these technology-related challenges in real time, bringing insight into how AI is shaping their learning, decision-making, and future aspirations.
  • Network with Innovators: Connect with educators, researchers, and industry partners who are committed to building a technologically enabled, student-centered future for learners in higher education.

Whether you are discovering new tools, experimenting with AI in your teaching or work, or contributing to the broader conversation about technology and education, the AI & the Future of Learning Summit offers a space to engage, connect, and move ideas forward.
